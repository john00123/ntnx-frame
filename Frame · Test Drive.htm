Frame Test Drive


Experience the full power of Frame with your free 2-hour Test Drive. You'll see your Windows software running in a browser, with no hardware and no cost.

After you set up your account, you'll receive a validation email within a few minutes (if not, check your spam folder). When you have set aside two hours for testing, click the link in the email to start up your account (the link stays active for 24 hours). The virtual machines in your account will then be created and powered on within 15 minutes. We'll send you another email when everything's ready to go.

For a step-by-step walkthrough check out our Getting Started Guide to get on track in no time. Please note that by using our free Test Drive you agree to the Test Drive Terms of Service.
